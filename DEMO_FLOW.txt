================================================================================
  5-MINUTE SCREEN RECORDING — DEMO FLOW
  KPI Monitoring System
================================================================================

Total time: ~5 minutes
Tip: Have the system already running at http://localhost/kpi_system/
     before you start recording. Use year 2026 for most demos.

================================================================================
  SEGMENT 1 — LOGIN  (0:00 – 0:20)
================================================================================

ACTION:
  - Open browser to http://localhost/kpi_system/
  - Show the login page briefly
  - Type: admin / admin123
  - Click Login

WHAT TO SHOW:
  - The branded login card with gradient header
  - The demo credentials section at the bottom

================================================================================
  SEGMENT 2 — SUPERVISOR DASHBOARD  (0:20 – 1:00)
================================================================================

ACTION:
  - Land on the dashboard
  - Point to the 4 summary cards (hover over each to show tooltip)
  - Scroll down to show the Attention Required alert
  - Show the gamified level distribution (Platinum/Gold/Silver/Bronze)
  - Point to the Performance Distribution doughnut chart (year in centre)
  - Show the Top Performers & At-Risk side panel
  - Briefly show the staff performance table at the bottom
  - Change the year filter to 2025 to show it updates

WHAT TO SHOW:
  - Clickable cards with hover animation
  - Year label in the centre of the doughnut chart
  - At-risk staff names with "View Profile" links

================================================================================
  SEGMENT 3 — KPI SCORE ENTRY  (1:00 – 1:30)
================================================================================

ACTION:
  - Click "KPI Score Entry" in the sidebar
  - Select any staff member (e.g. Bob Smith) and year 2026
  - Show the collapsible sections (click to expand a category)
  - Click a few score buttons (1–5) to show the colour-coded buttons
  - Point to the live sidebar: score updates in real time, doughnut animates
  - Show the progress bar filling as more scores are entered
  - Do NOT submit — just demonstrate the interface

WHAT TO SHOW:
  - Collapsible Core Competencies + 5 KPI categories
  - Colour-coded score buttons (red → green)
  - Real-time score calculation in the sidebar
  - Classification badge updating live

================================================================================
  SEGMENT 4 — ALL STAFF  (1:30 – 1:50)
================================================================================

ACTION:
  - Click "All Staff" in the sidebar
  - Show the staff card grid with initials + photo
  - Use the Performance Level dropdown to filter (e.g. "Top Performer")
  - Use the Staff Member dropdown to select one person
  - Click "View Full Profile" on any staff card

WHAT TO SHOW:
  - Staff photos on the right side of each card
  - Filter dropdowns working together
  - Card layout with score, progress bar, trend badge

================================================================================
  SEGMENT 5 — STAFF PROFILE  (1:50 – 2:20)
================================================================================

ACTION:
  - Land on the staff profile (from clicking View Full Profile)
  - Show the profile header: initials avatar + photo on right
  - Point to the Performance Story (auto-generated narrative)
  - Scroll to show the 3 charts:
      * Performance Trend line chart (multi-year)
      * Current Score gauge (doughnut)
      * KPI Category radar chart
  - Show the Strengths & Weaknesses progress bars
  - Click "Add/Edit Comment" to open the modal
  - Show the quick templates, select one
  - Close the modal (don't save)

WHAT TO SHOW:
  - All 3 charts with storytelling text below each
  - The Alpine.js comment modal with templates

================================================================================
  SEGMENT 6 — ANALYTICS  (2:20 – 2:50)
================================================================================

ACTION:
  - Click "Analytics" in the sidebar
  - Show the filter bar (Year, Department, Performance Level)
  - Point to each of the 5 charts as they load:
      1. Performance Distribution doughnut (year in centre)
      2. Department Comparison bar chart
      3. Multi-Year Trend line chart
      4. KPI Category Averages radar chart
      5. Score Distribution histogram
  - Point to the storytelling insight below each chart
  - Change the Department filter to show it updates

WHAT TO SHOW:
  - All 5 charts with dynamic storytelling
  - The info tooltip on "Average Score" card

================================================================================
  SEGMENT 7 — REPORTS  (2:50 – 3:15)
================================================================================

ACTION:
  - Click "Top Performers" in the sidebar
  - Show the table of top performers with scores
  - Click "At-Risk Staff"
  - Show the at-risk table with risk levels and reasons
  - Click "Training Needs"
  - Show the training recommendations table
  - Scroll down to show "Most Common Training Needs" with progress bars
  - Change the year to 2022 to show historical data

WHAT TO SHOW:
  - Three different report types
  - Most Common Training Needs keyword analysis with progress bars
  - Export CSV button

================================================================================
  SEGMENT 8 — PREDICTIVE ALERTS (TensorFlow.js)  (3:15 – 3:45)
================================================================================

ACTION:
  - Click "Predictive Alerts" in the sidebar
  - Show the TensorFlow.js badge in the header
  - Point to the threshold bar (Critical/High/Medium/Low ranges)
  - Wait for the SweetAlert loading modal to appear and complete
  - Show the 4 summary cards with counts and percentages
  - Point to the alert cards (Critical/High/Medium) with recommendations
  - Click the green "Low / Stable Staff" collapsed section to expand it
  - Click "Re-run Predictions" to show the SweetAlert loading again

WHAT TO SHOW:
  - TF.js training in the browser (SweetAlert loading)
  - Risk classification matching the threshold bar
  - Collapsed Low/Stable section

================================================================================
  SEGMENT 9 — TRAINING RECOMMENDATIONS  (3:45 – 4:00)
================================================================================

ACTION:
  - Click "Training Recommendations" in the sidebar
  - Click "Generate Recommendations"
  - Show a staff card with skill gap badges and match score
  - Click "Edit Comment" on one staff member
  - Show it redirects to the staff profile with the modal open
  - Go back

WHAT TO SHOW:
  - Skill gap badges (Critical/Moderate/Minor)
  - Match score percentage
  - Edit Comment → staff profile flow

================================================================================
  SEGMENT 10 — ANOMALY DETECTION  (4:00 – 4:15)
================================================================================

ACTION:
  - Click "Anomaly Detection" in the sidebar
  - Select a staff member from the dropdown (e.g. Alice Johnson)
  - Show the line chart with anomaly markers
  - Point to the anomaly cards (Spike / Drop / Decline)
  - Show the narrative insight text

WHAT TO SHOW:
  - Chart with visual anomaly markers
  - Plain-English narrative explaining what happened

================================================================================
  SEGMENT 11 — PEER COMPARISON  (4:15 – 4:40)
================================================================================

ACTION:
  - Click "Peer Comparison" in the sidebar
  - Select a staff member (e.g. Emma Wilson)
  - Set "Compare With" to "Auto-select Best Match"
  - Click Compare
  - Show the VS card layout with both staff scores (proper percentages)
  - Point to the radar chart comparing KPI categories
  - Show the similarity score and insights
  - Change "Compare With" to "Specific Staff Member"
  - Select a peer manually and compare again

WHAT TO SHOW:
  - Side-by-side comparison with animated VS badge
  - Radar chart with two datasets
  - Specific staff comparison option

================================================================================
  SEGMENT 12 — GAMIFICATION  (4:40 – 5:00)
================================================================================

ACTION:
  - Click "Dashboard" in the sidebar
  - Scroll to the gamified level distribution section
  - OR navigate to the gamification page if accessible
  - Show the podium (🥇🥈🥉) with top 3 staff
  - Show the full leaderboard table with points and levels
  - Show the badge showcase section

WHAT TO SHOW:
  - Animated counter on level cards
  - Podium layout
  - Points breakdown

================================================================================
  CLOSING  (5:00)
================================================================================

  End on the dashboard or the predictive alerts page.
  The TF.js badge and the charts make a strong visual finish.
